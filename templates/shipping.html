Shipping policies

Shipping within Nairobi

Shipping within Nairobi is done on the same day as long as you order before 6:00pm Note that additional courier charged will be added and it depends on your location.

Shipping outside Nairobi

Shipping outside Nairobi will be sent via parcel.It may take 2 to 3 working days.They charges of the parcels are to be paid by the buyer.

Shipping to Tanzania,Uganda

Shipping to Tanzania and Uganda will be done via parcel.They charges of the parcels are to be paid by the buyer.It may take 4 to 5 working days..
